The work addresses a key challenge in autonomous in-space assembly, which is critical for future large-scale space structures.

Downs' algorithm focuses on precise insertion tasks, a fundamental capability for robotic assembly in orbit. The collaboration with NASA and the U.S. Air Force highlights the practical relevance of the research for upcoming space missions.
