As the controversy enters its third week, the spotlight has shifted to the broader tech community, which now plays the role of jury in this high-stakes dispute. This clash underscores the growing tension between competing visions for AI’s role in society—whether it should be tightly controlled or openly accessible. As the jury—comprising peers, policymakers, and the public—deliberates, the implications for the robotics and AI sectors are profound.
