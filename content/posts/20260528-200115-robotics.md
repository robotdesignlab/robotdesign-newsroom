Japanese robotics developers are showcasing humanoid robots with advanced dexterity and mobility, aiming to compete with Chinese counterparts. Demonstrations include mechanical hands capable of threading a needle and childlike dancing robots, as well as adult-sized humanoids designed for delivery tasks.
