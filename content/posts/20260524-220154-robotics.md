UK-based Humanoid has partnered with Bosch to mass-produce its HMND humanoid robots for industrial applications. The collaboration aims to leverage Bosch's manufacturing expertise to scale production of the robots, which are designed for tasks such as logistics, warehousing, and assembly in manufacturing environments. The HMND robot features advanced mobility, dexterity, and AI-driven autonomy, enabling it to operate alongside human workers. Bosch will provide its global manufacturing infrastructure and supply chain capabilities to accelerate deployment. The partnership targets initial deployment in automotive and electronics industries, with plans to expand to other sectors. Financial terms were not disclosed.
