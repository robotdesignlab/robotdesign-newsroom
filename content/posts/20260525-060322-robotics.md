UBS analyst suggests that the repeat purchase rate is the most important metric for assessing the humanoid robot industry, as it reflects real market demand and product maturity. The analyst emphasizes that tracking whether customers buy additional units after initial deployment provides a clearer signal than shipment forecasts or prototype announcements, which can be misleading in the early-stage market.
